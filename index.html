SunsetHacks

a hack club hackathon.

Code, Connect, and Collect

Make Memories
Hackathons are the perfect playground for you to connect, team up, and collaborate on exciting projects. Whether you're looking to make new friends or engage in some friendly competition, our event is the place to be.
Create and Learn
While common hackathon projects include apps and websites, you're free to bring any idea to life. Our mentors are here to guide you, and we'll be hosting engaging workshops to enhance your skills. From coding to design, there's always something new to learn.
Get Free Stuff
Who doesn't love freebies? Dive into the hacking experience, and enjoy a bounty of snacks and swag available to all attendees. But that's not all – by showcasing your projects, you'll also have the chance to win awesome prizes!
