Code, Connect, and Collect

Make Memories
Hackathons are the perfect playground for you to connect, team up, and collaborate on exciting projects. Whether you're looking to make new friends or engage in some friendly competition, our event is the place to be.
Create and Learn
While common hackathon projects include apps and websites, you're free to bring any idea to life. Our mentors are here to guide you, and we'll be hosting engaging workshops to enhance your skills. From coding to design, there's always something new to learn.
Get Free Stuff
Who doesn't love freebies? Dive into the hacking experience, and enjoy a bounty of snacks and swag available to all attendees. But that's not all – by showcasing your projects, you'll also have the chance to win prizes!

FAQ

SunsetHacks is totally free!
High school students from the ages of 13-18 are eligible to attend.
You don't have to be an advanced coder to attend! We have workshops throughout the hackathon that teaches you how to code, even if it's your first line of code.
A team can be 1-4 people. Meaning, you are allowed to work alone if you want! If you don't have a team, no worries -- join our Discord and you can form a team.
At the moment, we unfortunately do not provide travel stipends, but you can join our Discord for updates. However, Hack Club is providing a reimbursement for gas and train/bus tickets: _.
Please do not hesitate to email _ for any questions or concerns of the hackathon. Alternatively, you can join our Discord for a quicker response.
