SunsetHacks

An incredible experience
at your fingertips.

SunsetHacks is a San Diego-based hackathon run for high schoolers, by high schoolers. We invite kids passionate about tech from all over the county to come together for 24 hours, where they can build software or hardware projects from scratch and compete for cool prizes. All experience levels are welcome!
